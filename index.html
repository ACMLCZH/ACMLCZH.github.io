About Me

I am a first-year MSc student in Computer Science at the University of Massachusetts Amherst, under the guidance of Prof. Chuang Gan. My research interests span Robotics, Computer Graphics, and Data Intelligence. Prior to my graduate studies, I earned my bachelor's degree from Peking University, where I was an undergraduate member of the Center on Frontiers of Computing Studies (CFCS), mentored by Prof. Hao Dong.
